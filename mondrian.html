Mondrian Painting

Modern or contemporary architecture in the 21st century is not characterized by any single dominant style. For the purposes of this analysis we will consider modern architecture in Boston to be any building or structure built since the year 2000. Generally speaking, this building style reflects an abstract interpretation of design. The structures are meant to astonish and bewilder, while also serving the purpose of a modern structure. Included in this analysis are some of the more striking examples of modern architecture in the Boston area.
