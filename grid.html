Grid Layout

Modern architecture in the 21st century, known as contemporary architecture, is not characterized by any single dominant style. The structures are meant to astonish and bewilder while also serving the purposes of a modern structure. Some appear as massive concrete sculptures brought to life with striking colors and irregular shapes. For the purposes of this analysis we will consider modern architecture as any building that broke ground after the year 2000. Generally speaking, this building style reflects an abstract interpretation of design. Included in this analysis are some of the more striking examples of contemporary architecture in the Boston area.
